Ugly Dogge Bullies LLC

About

Led by owner and trainer Nick Lamson, Ugly Dogge Bullies LLC offers dog training services for both pet dogs and working dogs. In addition to having attended Tarheel School for Dog Trainers, Nick trains for competitive Protection sports, scentwork and tracking.

Services

01.

BOARD AND TRAIN

2-week program which offers constructive obedience and socialization. This is ideal for puppies and young dogs, but is welcome to dogs of all ages.

02.

PRIVATE LESSONS

Reach out for a free evaluation to determine a fully customized obedience plan tailored to you and your dog.

03.

PROTECTION

Reach out for a free evaluation to see if your dog is a good candidate for this type of training.

Abbie & Cannoli

4 Week Board & Train Client

"I can't recommend Nick enough. He is great at what he does and you can see his passion while doing it."

My pup Cannoli and I started working with Nick when Cannoli was 7 months old. We did 3 1:1 sessions with Nick over the course of a week. During that week, Cannoli learned a lot, but I learned just as much. I continued to work with her on the things we had learned from Nick and she was doing well.

Then at 11 months old, I boarded Cannoli with Nick for a month. Leaving her with someone was really hard for me to do, but I was confident it would benefit her so much. I'm so happy to say that it did, even beyond my expectations!

Nick communicated with me daily and sent me videos and photos and updated me on what they were working on and how Cannoli was doing. The same puppy that was sad when I left her after drop-off, didn't even care I was leaving after my first visit. That brought me so much comfort to know how comfortable she was and how much she was enjoying the work.

Now at 1 yr old Cannoli has amazing recall, loads into her crate or place without hesitation, lays down, sits, and her leash manners are much better. Where Cannoli is at 1 yr took me 2 years to obtain with my first dog on my own.

Nick is the real deal. Dogs trust him and I do too. My Cannoli wasn't only trained in his care, but was loved, exercised, socialized, and he even had her tracking deer trails as a bonus.
