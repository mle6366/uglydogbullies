Ugly Dogge Bullies LLC

About

Led by owner and trainer Nick Lamson, Ugly Dogge Bullies LLC offers dog training services for both pet dogs and working dogs. In addition to having attended Tarheel School for Dog Trainers , Nick also trains for competitive Protection sports, scentwork and tracking.

Services

01.

BOARD AND TRAIN

2-week program which offers constructive obedience and socialization. This is ideal for puppies and young dogs, but is welcome to dogs of all ages.

02.

PRIVATE LESSONS

Reach out for a free evaluation to determine a fully customized obedience plan tailored to you and your dog.

03.

PROTECTION

Reach out for a free evaluation to see if your dog is a good candidate for this type of training. Or, if you already have a working dog, either in Law Enforcement, Personal Protection, or Sport, Nick offers decoy services.
